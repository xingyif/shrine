Results: Query Status View

The results of your query appear in the Query Status view. Information provided includes:

  • Query Name
  • Elapsed execution time
  • Aggregated Query Results from participating hospitals

If your query returned between zero (0) and 10 patients, your results display as None.

The results are time and date stamped.

Note: When you log out of SHRINE, your results are no longer available.
