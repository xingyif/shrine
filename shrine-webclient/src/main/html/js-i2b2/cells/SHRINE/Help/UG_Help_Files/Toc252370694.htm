Accessing SHRINE

To access SHRINE, you must complete the required registration and authentication process at your local institution.

Registering at Your Local Institution

To register for use of the SHRINE Beta release:

  • BIDMC – Go to your institution's registration page to start the process:
    https://bric.bidmc.harvard.edu/shrine
  • CHB – Go to your institution's registration page to start the process:
    https://i2b2msprod.tch.harvard.edu:7443/i2b2_shrine_user_reg/
  • BWH and MGH – Contact Shawn N. Murphy (snmurphy@partners.org) for more information.

Note: You must be behind your hospital's firewall to access SHRINE.

Complete the registration process as identified by your institution. This process might take a few minutes or a few days, depending on the specific verification process. After you are registered, you use the account credentials provided to access SHRINE.

Please use our Feedback form to contact the SHRINE team or to request assistance.
